CapEx App

Keep track of all your tangible assets with the Capex Pro tool.

A cloud-based Fixed Asset Records solution, Capex Pro connects financials to Assets Records with asset creation, logs, allocation and location of each asset along with date stamp, and capitalisation/depreciation details.

Our OCR based identification of Asset serial number is tallied with the serial number on the invoice for a two-way match.

The Dashboards provides overall data on capitalisation and depreciation of all assets.

FAQs

  • Why CFOPyramid?

    Whether you have just started out or are looking for scalability in your business, CFOPyramid supports all kind of businesses and can come up with tailor-made strategies to suit your business needs.

  • How do I know if I need CFOPyramid services?

    Here are a few things to consider while making your decision:
    1. How often do you review your financial performance?
    2. Are you able to fully understand your financial statements?
    3. Are you aware of cash in-flows and out-flows and their impact on your business?
    4. Do you need a budget framework that can help you align your business goals and growth?
    5. How can you ensure you are maximising your profitability?
    6. How much time are spending on core business activities as compared to time spent on financial analysis, bookkeeping, etc.

  • How experienced will my CFOPyramid be?

    For a growing business it makes sense to seek out dedicated financial support virtually when compared to hiring a full-time, in-house CFO.
    CFOPyramid manages your financials while you are free to refocus all your energies and efforts into growth and profitability.
